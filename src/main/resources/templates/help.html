The basic entity of the system is a VISIT. For instance, mister Theo is going on vacation and wants to leave his beloved cat Mruczek at the Pets Hotel for a while. Welcome, cat Mruczek! So, the process of a Mruczek's stay in the hotel is a VISIT. Our new visitor settles in a separate "room", which we call a BOX. Now the most important thing. Our visitor is an unusual guest! He is a cat! The cat needs care and attention: he needs to be fed, watered, and the box must be cleaned regularly... Each such activity (feeding, drinking, cleaning, combing, etc.) is called an EVENT in our system. And the aggregation of such events constitutes the SCRIPT of the VISIT.
